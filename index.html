• 2 slices of bread
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • cheddar
    • mozzarella
    • pepper jack
  • 1 tbsp of butter

Steps for creating grilled cheese

  1. Spread the butter on the bread and frying pan
  2. place the bread in frying pan and fly
  3. Add cheese on top of bread
  4. Cover with second slice of bread
  5. Turn over and fly for 2 minutes
